Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our shop, add your favorite items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to enter your details and confirm payment.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, mobile banking options, and secure online payment gateways.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–5 business days depending on your location. You’ll receive a tracking code once your order is shipped.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we have a hassle-free 7-day return and exchange policy. Please check our return guidelines for details.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us via the Contact Us page, email at support@alishamart.com, or call our hotline.
